Zhijindong Park to Become Internationally Recognized Global Geopark

We are elated to announce our new status as a UNESCO Global Geopark.


Zhijindong's awesome team of scientists, architects, builders and guest receptionists have done an amazing job helping with our efforts to revitalize our treasured park and put us on the world stage.


We updated our facilities to give Zhijindong a fresh look and undertook a massive construction project to build new learning centers. The construction of information centers and the Zhijindong Cave geo-museum, located in the main scenic area was paramount in helping us gain global geopark status. The architecutre is inspired by the Chinese Yi Nationality.


The total square area of the new museum is 2400 square meters, divided into several parts: the reception hall, the earth and science halls, the Guizhou plateau exhibit, the charming Zhijin village and ecology exhibits, and our cutting-edge 4D popular science theater.


The theater is our most popular addition, showing every detail of Guizhou's Karst caves and the evolution of Zhijindong Cave. We also display our region's ethnic customs and culture. Our main feature is a grand display of the value and unique charm of Zhijindong Cave in global Karst history


Our new museum is one of thousands of reasons we encourage tourists to visit and discover the secrets of China.

Zhijindong Park Website Gets A New Look

We hope you love it as much as we do.


In our efforts to let the world know we are here and help the local economy flourish, we consulted with an American web designer who gave our website a clean beautiful new look that will entice future tourists in China and abroad.


On the new website, you can view pictures of the geopark, get information on general admission prices and operating hours and reserve spots on our hourly tours. We also have an updated look for our park news, geology and Zhijin cultural pages. Each page features articles about events we hold here and the formation of Zhijin's karst structures, as well as background on the surrounding Yi minority villages and environment.


Our new website is mobile friendly, allowing you to check our hours and ticket prices any time anywhere, as well as make reservations for a tour. We also update our news feed regularly so you can always know what is happening around Zhijin first so you can get here before the crowds arrive for our unique festivals and once-in-a-lifetime events.

The theater is our most popular addition, showing every detail of Guizhou's Karst caves and the evolution of Zhijindong Cave. We also display our region's ethnic customs and culture. Our main feature is a grand display of the value and unique charm of Zhijindong Cave in global Karst history
